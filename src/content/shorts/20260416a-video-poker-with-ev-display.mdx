I created an 8-bit video poker game with 4 different payout themes, each with payout-specific Expected Values based on your hold cards and bet. I had a lot of fun building it and it is for sure my go to video poker game online.

Displaying the EV (Expected Value) of your hold cards gives you instant feedback on whether your hold card raises or lowers your expected payout (higher the better).
