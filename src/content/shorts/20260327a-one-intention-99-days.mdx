I built something small.

It’s called **99 Days Of** — a simple space to commit to one intention for 99 days.

- No accounts.
- No ads.
- No tracking.
- No noise.

Just a single idea you want to bring into your life, and a quiet place to follow through.

Each day is one dot.

That’s it.

You can add notes if you want, or keep it minimal. Over time, the dots become a visual record of your consistency — imperfect, but real.

Everything is stored locally in your browser and encrypted with a passphrase. Your data never leaves your device.

At the end, you get a simple artifact of your 99-day journey.

It’s not trying to optimize you.

It’s just a space to show up.
