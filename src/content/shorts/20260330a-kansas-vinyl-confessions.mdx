One of my earliest memories of this album is playing it on repeat on a pink microcassette player in the computer lab at Northern Michigan University.

It remains one of my absolute favorites and I listen to it in its entirety multiple times a year.

If you haven't had the pleasure, give a try [Vinyl Confessions on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/album/31kDP6XF3s8dIt0iGFS4PX?si=JjkpgOh_SCKARmxGSkeuMQ)
